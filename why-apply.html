Data Science by the numbers.

Just some of the statistics behind the profession.

The emergence of data

In the past three years alone, the amount of digital data created or captured online has doubled in size. In three years, it will double again.

The changes are not only in the of quantity but in the kind of data captured digitally -- as audio, video, images have become ubiquitous in our online world.

Increased demand for data science

The amount of data captured already exceeds the capacity of those in business or government to properly utilize it. Data scientists fill this gap by identifying the critical subset of data that drives meaningful impact.

The market has responded accordingly. December 2018 saw a 31% increase in data science job postings from one year earlier.

Data Analyst vs Data Scientist

What's the difference?

About $60,000.

On average, data scientists command 60k more than data analysts nationwide. Data scientists in NYC earned an average of 146k in 2018.

Also Python and statistics skills.

Most data scientist positions require coding skills in Python as well as statistics skills. Most data analyst positions do not.
